Section 01

Product Overview

HR Interview Bot is an autonomous AI recruiting agent that conducts voice-based interviews, performs deep candidate research, and delivers transparent, criteria-based rankings — eliminating the screening bottleneck while ensuring every candidate receives a consistent, professional evaluation.

Unlike basic resume parsers or one-way video tools, our bot conducts live adaptive interviews, cross-references public data, and explains exactly why a candidate scored the way they did.

Section 03

Feature Deep-Dive

3.1

Intelligent Dashboard & Candidate Management

What it is: A centralized command center where teams view all candidates, track pipeline status, and take action in real-time.
  • Real-time pipeline metrics: Total, Pending, Passed, Failed at a glance
  • Smart filtering by status and role type
  • Flexible sorting by AI match score or recency
  • One-click workflows: resend invites, schedule, deep-dive
Your entire candidate pipeline, visible and actionable in one screen. No more scattered spreadsheets or chasing interview notes."
3.2

Transparent AI Ranking & Matching

What it is: The bot calculates relevance based on specific Job Description and Title, then explains the scoring weightage transparently.
  • Technical Competency: ~50% weightage
  • Communication Skills: ~33% (25–50% based on role)
  • Leadership & Team Bonding: Variable by seniority
  • Calibrated scoring (75% when JD matched resume exactly)
No black boxes. Our AI shows exactly how it weighed technical skills, communication, and leadership against your specific job description."
3.3

Deep Candidate Research (Internet Intelligence)

What it is: Automated deep dive across the open web to find publicly accessible references — publications, presentations, conference talks, and more.
  • Cross-references findings with resume to verify identity
  • Prioritizes guaranteed sources (GitHub, LinkedIn, portfolios)
  • Clearly flags verified vs. discovered information
  • Avoids confusion between same-name candidates
Know your candidate beyond the resume. Surface public mentions, publications, and presentations cross-checked for accuracy."
3.4

Proctoring & Interview Integrity

What it is: Multi-layer security ensuring the interview represents the real candidate without cheating.
  • Tab Switching Detection: Auto-terminates after 3+ switches
  • Camera Detection: Verifies presence throughout
  • Voice Anomaly Detection: Identifies inconsistencies
  • Comprehensive integrity scoring
Interview integrity built in. Auto-termination, camera checks, and voice anomaly detection ensure scores reflect the real candidate."
3.5

Interview Experience & Flow

What it is: Natural, professional interview experience with proper introductions and conversational flow.
  • Proper self-introduction: "Hello, I'm your AI interviewer today…"
  • Readiness check before starting questions
  • Natural voice-based interaction (not robotic forms)
  • Adaptive questioning based on responses
Candidates talk to a professional interviewer, not a chatbot. Natural voice interaction that leaves candidates feeling heard."
3.6

Flexible Scheduling

What it is: 95% autonomous scheduling with manual override capability for special cases.
  • Auto-scheduling for standard roles
  • Manual scheduling links for senior/executive candidates
  • Multi-channel delivery: Email, WhatsApp, SMS
  • Configurable link expiry (default: 72 hours)
Autonomous for volume, human-controlled for VIPs. Bulk interviews on autopilot, manual controls when hiring executives."
3.7

Performance & Scale

Current Capacity: Configured for 50 resumes per cycle, scaling to 100 with active load testing.
Built to scale. From startup hiring sprints to enterprise volume, infrastructure is load-tested with transparent cost structure."
